刘洋

作者：来源：发布时间：2017-04-14

个人简介

刘洋，男，1982年9月出生，浙江省常山县人，现为浙江师范大学数理与信息工程学院副教授、硕士生导师。

学习经历

1999.9-2003.7  浙江师范大学数学系获理学学士，数学专业;

2003.9-2008.3  同济大学数学系获理学博士学位，基础数学专业；

2014.1-2014.3  受澳门大学邀请资助将赴澳门大学合作研究；

2014.9-2015.8  受国家留学基金委和教育厅资助将赴普渡大学访学。

工作简历

2008.3-至今    浙江师范大学数理与信息工程学院任教；

讲师（2008.9）/副教授（2010.9）硕导（2010.10）

学术兼职

美国数学评论评论员(2013.1-至今)

人才梯队、荣誉

上海市优秀博士论文获得者(2012.2);

浙江省高校中青年学科带头人(2013.5)；

浙江省151人才工程第三层次(2013.9)。

研究领域及成果

从事多复变函数论、Clifford分析和系统控制理论及相关领域的研究工作。近年来，在TOP期刊《IEEE Trans. Autom. Control》、《IEEE Trans. Fuzzy Syst.》、《Intern. J. Control》以及《J. Math. Anal. Appl.》、《Intern. J. Rob. Nonl. Control》、《Intern. J. Adapt. Control Signal Proc.》、《ESAIM：COCV》、《Science in China：A》、《J. Comput. Appl. Math.》、《Nonl. Anal. RWA》、《Math. Comp. Mod.》、《Int. J. Bifurcat. Chaos》、《Intern. J. Syst. Science》等杂志上发表论文30余篇。

代表性论文、论著
  1. Schwarz-Pick estimates for bounded holomorphic functions in the unit ball of C^n. Acta Math. Sin. B, 26(5), 2010.
  2. Schwarz-Pick estimates for positive real part holomorphic functions on unit ball and polydisc. Science in China A, 53(4) 2010.
  3. Schwarz-Pick estimates for holomorphic mappings from the polydisk to the unit ball. J Math. Anal. Appl., 376, 2011.
  4. High-order Green operators on the disk and the polydisc. Methods and Applications of Analysis, 2014.
  5. A variant of Hörmander’s L^2 theorem for Dirac operator in Clifford analysis. J Math. Anal. Appl., 410, 2014.
  6. Controllability for a Class of Linear Time-Varying Impulsive Systems With Time Delay in Control Input. IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, 56, 2011.
  7. A New Fuzzy Impulsive Control of Chaotic Systems Based on T-S Fuzzy Model. IEEE Transactions on Fuzzy Systems, 19(2), 2011.
  8. Controllability analysis of linear time-varying systems with multiple time delays and impulsive effects. Nonlinear Analysis: RWA,13, 2012.
  9. Some necessary and sufficient conditions for the controllability of temporal Boolean control networks. ESAIM: Control, Optimisation and Calculus of Variations, 2014.
  10. Admissibility and static output-feedback stabilization of singular Markovian jump systems with defective statistics of modes transitions, Int. J. Robust Nonlinear Control,2013.

2.2010－2010,国家自然科学基金天元项目－“多复变中的逆紧全纯映射和全纯函数的Schwarz-Pick估计”；

3.2010－2012,浙江省自然科学基金一般项目－“多复变中全纯映射的 Schwarz-Pick 估计和复系统的定性理论研究”。

学术报告

1.2012.9,全国多复变研讨会，邀请报告《High-order Green operators on the disk and the polydisc》；

2.2013.11,第七届复杂系统与网络科学研究中心论坛，邀请报告《Solvability and controllability of singular Boolean networks》。

近三年教学情况

为本科生主讲课程有《数学分析》、《高等数学》，近三年本科教学考核A，指导本科生论文20余篇；为研究生开设《时滞系统理论》、《脉冲控制理论》等课程，指导培养硕士研究生6名。指导国家级大学生创新创业计划项目1项，指导浙江省新苗计划一项。指导大学生获浙江省电子设计竞赛二等奖1项。

Profile

Liu Yang, male, born in September 1982, Changshan County, Zhejiang Province, who is now an Associate Professor and a Master Instructor in the College of Mathematics and Information Engineering, Zhejiang Normal University.

Education Background

1999.9-2003.7, Department of Mathematics, Zhejiang Normal University, Bachelor degree in mathematics;

2003.9-2008.3, Department of Mathematics, Tongji University, PHD degree in mathematics;

2014.1-2014.3, Collaborative Research at University of Macau, supported by University of Macau;

2014.9-2015.8, Visiting Scholar at Purdue University, supported by the national CSC.

Work Experiences

2008.3-now, he teaches at the College of Mathematics and Information Engineering, Zhejiang Normal University;

Lecturer (2008.9)/Associate Professor (2010.9)/Master Instructor (2010.10)

Other Academic Positions/Memberships

He is engaged in several complex variables, Clifford analysis and systems control theory. In recent years, he has published over 30 papers at TOP journals including 《IEEE Trans. Autom.
