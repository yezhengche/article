师资队伍
刘洋的个人简历

刘洋，女，出生于1987年9月5日，2015年于荷兰莱顿大学取得博士学位，现就职于昆明理工大学环境科学与工程学院，环境科学系，副教授，硕士生导师，学院外事秘书。电话：18313813905，邮箱：minipig6@163.com。

主要研究方向：污染物生态毒性效应评价与预测，混合污染物毒性作用机理，纳米材料的环境行为及健康风险评价，天然有机质与污染物的相互作用，生物炭中自由基的生物效应。

主要研究成果：

Zhao J., Liu Y.*, Pan B., Gao G., Liu Y., Liu S., Liang N., Zhou D., Vijver M.G., Peijnenburg W.J.G.M., 2017. Tannic acid promotes ion release of copper oxide nanoparticles: Impacts from solution pH change and complexation reactions. Water Research 127, 59-67.

Liu Y., Vijver M.G., Pan B.*, Peijnenburg W.J.G.M., 2017. Toxicity models of metal mixtures established on the basis of “additivity” and “interactions”. Frontiers of Environmental Science & Engineering 11(2), 10.

Liu Y.*, Baas J., Peijnenburg W.J.G.M., Vijver M.G., 2016. Evaluating the combined toxicity of Cu and ZnO nanoparticles: utility of the concept of additivity and a nested experimental design. Environmental Science & Technology 50(10), 5328-5337.

Liu Y.*, Vijver M.G., Qiu H., Baas J., Peijnenburg W.J.G.M., 2015. Statistically significant deviations from additivity: What do they mean in assessing toxicity of mixtures? Ecotoxicology and Environmental Safety 122, 37-44.

Qiu H., Vijver M.G., He E.*, Liu Y., Wang P., Xia B., Smolders E., Versieren L., Peijnenburg W.J.G.M., 2015. Incorporating bioavailability into toxicity assessment of Cu-Ni, Cu-Cd, and Ni-Cd mixtures with the extended biotic ligand model and the WHAM-Ftox approach. Environmental Science & Pollution Research 22(23), 19213-19223.

Liu Y.*, Vijver M.G., Peijnenburg W.J.G.M., 2014. Comparing three approaches in extending biotic ligand models to predict the toxicity of binary metal mixtures (Cu–Ni, Cu–Zn and Cu–Ag) to lettuce (Lactuca sativa L.). Chemosphere 112, 282-288.

Liu Y.*, Vijver M.G., Peijenenburg W.J.G.M., 2014. Impacts of major cations (K+, Na+, Ca2+, Mg2+) and protons on toxicity predictions of nickel and cadmium to lettuce (Lactuca sativa L.) using exposure models. Ecotoxicology 23(3), 385-395.

徐凡, 马承伟, 曲梅, 刘洋, 宫彬彬, 张建宇, 曹晏飞, 孙国涛, 刘晨霞. 2014. 华北五省区日光温室微气候环境调查与评价. 中国农业气象, 35(1), 17-25.

徐凡, 马承伟, 刘洋, 宋道林. 2013. 天津一种典型砖墙日光温室热环境现状的测试与分析. 中国农业大学学报, 18(4), 188-195.

徐凡, 刘洋, 马承伟. 2010. 天津地区典型日光温室使用现状调查. 北方园艺, 15, 19-24.

工程纳米材料对土壤微生物多样性的影响（省教育厅资助性项目）

溶解有机质作用下氧化铜纳米颗粒的溶解释放（国家自然科学基金）
