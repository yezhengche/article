张勇（纳米生物工程）

作者：来源：发布时间：2017-04-14

个人简介

张勇，现为浙江师范大学客座教授。

学习经历

1988.9-1998.7   浙江大学材料系获工程学士、博士学位，无机专业

1998.10-2002.10 在葡萄牙国立生物医学工程研究所、华盛顿大学从事博士后研究工作

工作简历

2002.10-至今   新加坡国立大学工程学院任教

2012.1-至今    浙江师范大学生化学院任教（客座教授）

学术兼职

中国科学技术大学客座教授(2012.1-至今)

人才梯队、荣誉

浙江省千人计划短期项目海鸥计划（2013.3）

国家自然科学基金委员会海外与港澳学者合作研究基金项目(2013.8)

研究领域及成果

主要研究工作包括纳米材料和微器件的研究开发和应用，获得多项研究经费和科研奖励，在《Nature Medicine》、《Nature Communications》、《PNAS》等杂志上发表论文100余篇，出版著作8部。

代表性论文、论著

1. 论文“Rotational separation of non-spherical bioparticles using I-shaped pillar arrays in a microfluidic device ” 2013年发表于《Nature Communications》第4期；

2. 论文“In vivo photodynamic therapy using upconversion nanoparticles as remote controlled nano-transducers” 2012年发表于《Nature Medicine》第18期；

3. 论文“Remote activation of biomolecules in deep tissues using near-infrared-to-UV upconversion nanotransducers.” 2012年发表于《PNAS》第109期.

代表性科研项目

2013－2015,国家自然科学基金海外与港澳学者合作研究基金项目

学术报告

1.2013.7:在ICMAT2013研讨会作邀请报告《Upconverting Fluorescent Nanoparticles For Bioimaging and Cancer Therapy》

2.2012.1:在SPIE Photonics West研讨会作邀请报告《Small NIR-to-VIS upconverting nanoparticles for photodynamic therapy》

近三年教学情况

指导培养硕士研究生2名

Profile

Yong Zhang, Visiting Professor of College of Chemistry and Life Sciences

Education Background

1988.9-1998.7  BEng, PhD, Department of Materials Science & Engineering, Zhejiang University

1998.10-2002.10  Post-doctoral Fellow, National Institute of Biomedical Engineering (INEB), Portugal & University of Washington

Work Experiences

2002.10 -   Assistant Professor, Associate Professor & Professor, Faculty of Engineering, National University of Singapore

2013.1 -    Visiting Professor, College of Chemistry and Life Sciences, Zhejiang Normal University

2012.1 -    Guest Professor, University of Science & Technology of China

Honorable Titles and Awards:

Zhejiang 1000 Talent Program, short term, 2013.3

NSFC Joint Research Fund for Overseas, Hong Kong and Macao Young Scholars, 2013.8

Research Field and Achievements

Nanomaterials & Microfluidic Devices
Has made a significant impact in these fields as proved by many high-quality publications in top tier international journals such as Nature Medicine, Nature Communications, PNAS, etc., and many keynote/invited talks in international conferences.

Representative Works

1. Kwek K Ranjan S, Zhang Y. Nature Communications 2013, 4, 1625.

2. Idris NM, Jayakumar MKG, Zhang J, Ho PC, Mahendran R, Zhang Y. Nature Medicine 2012, 18, 1580-1585.

3. Jayakumar MKG, Idris NM & Zhang Y. Remote activation of biomolecules in deep tissues using near-infrared-to-UV upconversion nanotransducers. Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America (PNAS) 2012, 109, 8483-8488.

Research Projects

2013－2015，NSFC Joint Research Fund for Overseas, Hong Kong and Macao Young Scholars

Reports

1. Upconverting Fluorescent Nanoparticles For Bioimaging and Cancer Therapy, ICMAT2013, July 2013

2. Small NIR-to-VIS upconverting nanoparticles for photodynamic therapy, SPIE Photonics West, Jan 2012

Teaching and Supervision during Last 3 Years

2 MSc students
